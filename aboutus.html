About Us

Mission

  • To Develop World Largest Visual Rich Digital Content Database for all formal and non-formal streams of Technical Education and Deliver Across The Globe.

LearnEngg

  • Technology-enabled education is considered a promising solution for making the students learn effectively and become Industry-Ready ,We developed a unique innovative educational platform called “LearnEngg” (www.LearnEngg.com). It is a Visual Rich Digital Content demonstrating each and every Engineering topics using real-world applications with 3D/2D demonstrations.
  • It delivers “Visual Based Knowledge System” which addresses the industry readiness of the students and their understanding of Engineering concepts. It enhances knowledge and employability. It is specifically developed for Graduate Engineering, Polytechnic, ITI and Skill Development.
  • Our technology enabled solution is the BEST at least-cost for capacity building of vital resources and to thin-down the RURAL-URBAN divide through bridging the gap between Industry and Institutes.
  • The objective is to create conducive and effective learning / Training environment and accentuate the visual expression and thinking of students, virtually brings industry into the classroom.
  • We built Visual Based Knowledge System in last 10 years by a large team of Professors, Industrial Consultants and Developers with academic credibility.
  • It integrates skill development of student along with their academics.
  • It is a path breaking approach in streamlining the curriculum towards the industrial requirements.

Main Characteristics of LearnEngg

  • Delivered as per respective course syllabus.
  • A typical 45 hours course content (or subject) is explained by 200+ visual demonstrations. The Visuals are aptly embedded across the lecture notes from the start to end of the course.
  • It is available for B.E/B.Tech/Polytechnic/ITI subjects/Trades of major branches of Engineering namely, Mechanical, EEE, ECE, Instrumentation, Civil, IT, Computer Science, Automobile, Metallurgy, Chemical and their allied branches.
