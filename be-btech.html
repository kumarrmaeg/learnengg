B.E/B.TECH 4 YEARS ENGINEERING PROGRAM

COURSE OBJECTIVES

B.E/B.Tech 4 years engineering program development objectives are to provide a strong foundation and knowledge in engineering fundamentals through 3D/2D demonstrations with a capacity to know how, when and where to use the knowledge in specific ways.


Point by point content explanation with solved problems and images increase the ability to identify, formulate, analyse and solve engineering problems and a capacity to integrate material from more than one subject and to apply appropriate engineering principles to arrive at correct and effective solutions.
